# Operative Score Rubric (100 points)

## Hard failures — automatic veto

- Missing or zero-byte final file.
- Missing audio or video stream.
- Copyright or licensing uncertainty for a required asset.
- Undisclosed realistic synthetic media when disclosure is required.
- Fabricated citation, trend metric, testimonial, analytics result, link, product, or visual inspection.
- Thumbnail or metadata materially misrepresents the video.

## Weighted dimensions

| Dimension | Points | Evidence |
|---|---:|---|
| Editorial originality and usefulness | 20 | Script review and comparison notes |
| Visual relevance and scene variety | 20 | Sampled frames at opening, quartiles, and CTA |
| Thumbnail hierarchy and mobile legibility | 15 | Full-size and 10% preview |
| Audio intelligibility and mix | 15 | Loudness probe plus listening check |
| Technical integrity | 15 | Media probe and playback |
| Rights, provenance, and disclosure | 10 | Asset manifest and upload setting |
| Automated image metrics | 5 | Contrast, palette, sharpness; advisory only |

Pass threshold: 85/100, with no hard failures. Thumbnail threshold: 90/100 on a separate thumbnail checklist. Scores must not be rounded up to pass.
