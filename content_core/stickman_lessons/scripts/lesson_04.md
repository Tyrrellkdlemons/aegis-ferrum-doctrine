# LESSON_04 — Cheap Pleasure Steals Your Frame

**Series:** Ferrum Doctrine Stickman Combat Lessons  
**Length:** ~55s · Vertical 9:16  
**Brand sting:** Ferrum

## HOOK (0–3s)
You cannot lead a room when your nervous system is rented by cheap hits.

## LESSON (3–45s)
Porn, endless games, micro-rewards — they do not relax you. They flatten your hunger. A man with a rented reward system cannot hold frame, finish hard work, or tolerate boredom. That is not freedom. That is sedation.

## DEMAND (45–55s)
Thirty days without your cheapest hit. Or stay sedated. Decide.
