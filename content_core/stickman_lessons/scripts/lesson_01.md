## HOOK
Your comfort is not rest. It is a cage you decorate.

## LESSON (canon)
Every easy hit — scroll, snack, snooze — trains you to flinch from friction. Soft men call it balance. Operators call it surrender. You do not escape a cage by polishing the bars.

## DEMAND
Cut one comfort ritual tomorrow morning. Miss it and you chose the cage. Decide.
