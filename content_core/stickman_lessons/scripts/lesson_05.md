# LESSON_05 — Speak Second

**Series:** Ferrum Doctrine Stickman Combat Lessons  
**Length:** ~55s · Vertical 9:16  
**Brand sting:** Ferrum

## HOOK (0–3s)
Speak first and you lose. Read first and you own the next five minutes.

## LESSON (3–45s)
Soft men talk to soothe insecurity. Operators gather terrain — exits, status, anxiety, unspoken rule. Ten seconds of silence beats ten minutes of noise. Social power is perception under pressure, not charm.

## DEMAND (45–55s)
Run a ten-second room scan before you speak — seven days. Or stay loud and weak. Decide.

Ferrum.
