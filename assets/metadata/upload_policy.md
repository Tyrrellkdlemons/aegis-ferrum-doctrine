# YouTube Upload Policy for This Workspace

- Use the verified channel session only.
- Upload the exact file named in the publish packet after its hash and media probe are recorded.
- Select “Yes” for altered/synthetic content when realistic generated scenes are used in a way covered by YouTube’s disclosure policy.
- Do not enable mid-rolls unless the channel is eligible and the video length supports them.
- Do not promise affiliate products, a free PDF, a course, a community, or a pinned-comment funnel until the destination exists and has been checked.
- Do not publish AI personas as medical, legal, or financial experts.
- Save the video URL, privacy state, scheduled time, and disclosure selection to `comms_log.md` immediately after upload.
